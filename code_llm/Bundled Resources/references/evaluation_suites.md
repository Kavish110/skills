Evaluation suites (HELM, MT-Bench, MMLU)

- HELM: https://arxiv.org/abs/2203.06217
- MT-Bench: https://mt-bench.ai/ (public benchmark overview)
- MMLU: https://arxiv.org/abs/2009.03300
- Summary: Standardized benchmarks for capability, reasoning, and alignment across many tasks.
- What worked: Multi-dimensional evaluation helps detect regressions and compare models systematically.
- What didn't: Single metrics can be misleading; benchmarks can be gamed and may not reflect real-world user behavior.
