Chinchilla (Hoffmann et al., 2022)

- ArXiv: https://arxiv.org/abs/2203.15556
- Summary: Demonstrates that smaller models trained on more data can outperform larger undertrained models, emphasizing data-efficiency.
- What worked: Rebalanced compute allocation toward more training tokens for optimal loss.
- What didn't: Shows that overparameterizing without sufficient data is a poor strategy.
